## Overview

This post refers to the academic work **“Evolution of the Ransomware Phenomenon: Analysis of the Cybergangs Business Model”**, developed by **Giulio Santabarbara** within the research activities focused on ransomware ecosystems and cybercriminal business dynamics at the University of Sannio.

The work examines the evolution of ransomware groups from a business-model perspective, with attention to organizational structures, monetization strategies, and the economic mechanisms that sustain cybercriminal operations. This perspective helps frame ransomware not only as a technical threat, but also as an adaptive and profit-driven ecosystem.
