## Overview

This post refers to the academic work **“Dark Web Monitor: Evolution of a Threat Intelligence Platform for Monitoring the Dark Web to Intercept Data Leakage”**, developed by **Raffaela Crisci** within the research activities connected to Cyber Threat Intelligence and dark web monitoring at the University of Sannio.

The project focuses on the evolution of a threat intelligence platform designed to monitor underground sources and support the early identification of potential data leakage events. Its objective is to connect technical collection, source monitoring, and intelligence analysis in a structured workflow for investigating exposed information in dark web environments.
