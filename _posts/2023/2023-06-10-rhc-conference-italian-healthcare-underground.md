## Overview

At **Red Hot Cyber Conference 2023**, held in Cittaducale, near Rieti, I delivered a talk titled **“L’impronta lasciata dalla sanità italiana all’interno delle underground”**.

The session focused on the exposure of Italian healthcare-related data within underground cybercriminal ecosystems, with attention to dark web sources, data leakage dynamics, and the intelligence value of monitoring adversarial environments.

The talk was delivered by **Pietro Melillo**, CISO at **Würth Italia**, with a focus on the intersection between Cyber Threat Intelligence, healthcare-sector exposure, and underground data markets.
