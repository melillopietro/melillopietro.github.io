## Overview

This talk, titled **“I dati della sanità italiana all’interno delle underground”**, was delivered during **LinuxDay AV 2023**.

The session addressed the presence of healthcare-related Italian data within underground cybercriminal environments, discussing how dark web monitoring and Cyber Threat Intelligence can support the identification, contextualization, and analysis of exposed information.
