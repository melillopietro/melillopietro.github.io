## Overview

This post refers to the academic work **“Design and Implementation of a Threat Intelligence Platform for the Analysis of the Ransomware Phenomenon”**, developed by **Valerio Cammarota** within the research activities connected to Cyber Threat Intelligence and ransomware ecosystem analysis at the University of Sannio.

The project focuses on the design and implementation of a threat intelligence platform aimed at collecting, organizing, and analyzing information related to ransomware activity. The work contributes to a more structured understanding of ransomware operations by combining platform engineering, data collection, and intelligence-oriented analysis.
