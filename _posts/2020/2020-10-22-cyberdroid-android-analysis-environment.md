## Overview

**CyberDroid** is an academic project focused on the design and implementation of an environment for analyzing Android applications.

The project addresses the need for structured analysis workflows in mobile security, supporting the inspection of Android applications from a malware analysis and defensive research perspective. It represents an early academic contribution to the study of Android security and application behavior analysis.
