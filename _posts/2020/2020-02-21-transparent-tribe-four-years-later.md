## Overview

This post references the research article **“Transparent Tribe: Four Years Later”**, published by Yoroi.

The analysis focuses on the evolution of Transparent Tribe, an advanced persistent threat actor associated with cyber-espionage activity. The work examines the actor’s operational continuity, tooling, and observed tradecraft, contributing to a broader understanding of long-running APT campaigns and their adaptation over time.
