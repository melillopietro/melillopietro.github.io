## Overview

During the **Red Hot Cyber Conference 2025**, I delivered the workshop **“Come entrare nel Dark Web in sicurezza e accenni di CTI”**.

The session was designed to introduce participants to a safe and methodologically sound approach to dark web access, with a specific focus on operational security, investigative discipline, and the basic principles of Cyber Threat Intelligence.

## Workshop Focus

The workshop covered the main aspects that should be considered before accessing hidden services or underground environments, including:

- operational security and identity separation;
- safe browser and system configurations;
- risks associated with dark web navigation;
- intelligence collection boundaries;
- introductory CTI concepts applied to underground sources.

The objective was not to encourage casual exploration, but to explain how analysts, researchers, and security professionals can approach this environment in a controlled and responsible way.

## Professional Context

The session was delivered by **Pietro Melillo**, CISO at Würth Italia and Director of the DarkLab threat intelligence group at Red Hot Cyber.

The workshop reflected the same approach adopted in my academic and professional activities: combining technical awareness, intelligence methodology, and responsible handling of high-risk information sources.
