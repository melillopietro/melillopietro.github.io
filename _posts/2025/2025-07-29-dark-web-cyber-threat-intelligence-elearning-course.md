The **Dark Web & Cyber Threat Intelligence** e-learning course by [Red Hot Cyber Academy](https://academy.redhotcyber.com/courses/darkwebcti) introduces the core concepts of Cyber Threat Intelligence and the dark web ecosystem through structured, on-demand learning.

It is designed for students, junior analysts, IT professionals, and investigators who want to build a solid foundation before moving into more advanced operational activities.

## Course Format

The course is self-paced and includes video lessons, practical demonstrations, downloadable material, and quizzes. This format allows participants to progress gradually while consolidating the main concepts through examples and applied scenarios.

## Key Learning Areas

### Foundations of Cyber Threat Intelligence

- Definitions and intelligence objectives
- Intelligence lifecycle
- Threat actors, campaigns, and indicators
- Introduction to MITRE ATT&CK and the Lockheed Martin Cyber Kill Chain

### Dark Web Fundamentals

- Tor network and hidden services
- Deep web vs. dark web
- Marketplaces, forums, and underground communities
- Main risks associated with unstructured access

### Cybercrime Ecosystem

- Malware services and underground tooling
- Initial Access Brokers
- Infostealers and credential theft
- Cryptocurrency use in cybercriminal operations

### Tools and Techniques

- Basic OSINT methods applied to dark web contexts
- Safe access principles
- Monitoring and analysis of underground environments

## Target Audience

This course is suitable for:

- Cybersecurity students and enthusiasts
- Junior SOC analysts
- Threat intelligence beginners
- IT professionals interested in CTI
- Law enforcement and investigation professionals

## Instructor

The course is delivered by **Pietro Melillo**, CISO at Würth Italia and Director of the DarkLab CTI group at Red Hot Cyber.

The lessons are based on a combination of operational experience, academic research, and practical intelligence workflows.
