## Overview

This talk, delivered in the context of the **Digital Security Festival 2022**, focused on the role of **Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)** in understanding, prioritizing, and responding to modern cyber threats.

The session presented intelligence as a practical discipline that connects technical indicators, adversary behavior, risk analysis, and decision-making. The objective was to show how CTI can support both operational security activities and strategic cyber risk management.
