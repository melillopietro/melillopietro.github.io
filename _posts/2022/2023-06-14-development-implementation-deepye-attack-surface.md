## Overview

This academic project, developed by **Luca Ciarlo**, focused on the design and implementation of **Deepye**, a Threat Intelligence Platform intended to support attack surface analysis and the collection of cyber threat intelligence from open and closed sources.

The work fits within a broader research line on attacker-centric intelligence, where external exposure, observable infrastructure, and threat-related evidence are analyzed to support risk assessment and security prioritization.
