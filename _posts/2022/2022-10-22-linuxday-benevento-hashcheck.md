## Overview

At **LinuxDay Benevento 2022**, **Pietro Melillo** and **Vincenzo Di Lello** presented **HashCheck**, a utility designed to support hash verification and validation workflows.

The session focused on the role of cryptographic hashes in software integrity, file validation, and the verification of digital artifacts. The talk highlighted how simple and transparent tools can reduce operational errors when comparing, validating, and documenting hash values in technical and investigative contexts.
