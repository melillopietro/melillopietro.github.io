About Me

I currently serve as Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) at Würth Italy, where I oversee the cybersecurity strategy for Würth Italia, Würth Modyf, and KBlue Srl, ensuring alignment with business goals and regulatory frameworks such as GDPR and ISO/IEC 27001. My expertise lies in cyber threat intelligence, incident response, and security governance, with a focus on reducing risk and enhancing organizational resilience through threat-informed decision-making.

Alongside my corporate responsibilities, I am actively involved in academia as a lecturer in Cyber Threat Intelligence at the University of Rome Tor Vergata and other institutions, contributing to the development of next-generation cybersecurity professionals. As a Ph.D. researcher in Cyber Threat Intelligence at the University of Sannio, my work investigates threat actor behaviors, ransomware business models, and dark web ecosystems. I have authored research on threat detection platforms and underground market analysis, and regularly speak at conferences on emerging trends in cybercrime.

Research

My recent research focuses on infostealers, ransomware, and threat intelligence platforms. I actively contribute to conferences and publish works on cyber threats, underground ecosystems, and the evolution of malware economy.
