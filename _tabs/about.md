## Executive Summary

I am a cybersecurity professional and PhD researcher operating at the intersection of cyber threat intelligence, ransomware analysis, and enterprise security governance. My work bridges academic research with real-world operations, focusing on threat actor behavior, attack patterns, and large-scale data analysis to support risk-based decision making.

---

## Professional Leadership

I currently serve as the **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** for **Würth Italia Group Entities**, leading enterprise cybersecurity strategy, IT governance, and secure digital transformation. 

My operational focus includes:
* Designing and executing multi-year roadmaps for NIS2 compliance, covering supply-chain security, incident handling, and business continuity.
* Establishing enterprise AI governance frameworks aligned with the EU AI Act, including risk classification, AI DPIA, and model monitoring.
* Operationalizing a CTI-led security model by integrating SIEM, threat hunting, and the IoC/IoA lifecycle.
* Driving vulnerability management programs using risk-based prioritization and ensuring compliance with ISO/IEC 27001.

## Research & Academic Activity

As a **PhD Researcher in Cyber Threat Intelligence** at the **University of Sannio**, my work focuses on the ecosystem dynamics and structural resilience of the ransomware economy. I build data-driven models to interpret adversarial behavior, extortion evolution, and cybercriminal business dynamics.

Additionally, I serve as an active lecturer for Master's programs in Cybersecurity at institutions including the **University of Rome Tor Vergata** and the **University of Sannio**, delivering advanced modules on CTI, dark web intelligence, and ransomware ecosystems.
